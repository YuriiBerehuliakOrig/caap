# Getting Started

Let's start your CAAP journey. There's a lot to learn, but every journey starts
somewhere. In this chapter we'll discuss:

- What CAAP actually *is* — its layered design and why it is shaped that way.
- Building the `caap` command-line tool from this workspace.
- Writing and running your first program.
- The three ways CAAP turns your source into something that runs.

By the end of the chapter you'll have run real CAAP code and you'll understand
the vocabulary the rest of the book depends on: *forms*, the *kernel*, the
*bootstrap tower*, and *surfaces*.
